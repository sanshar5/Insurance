1. Gurans Savadhik Jeevan Beema Yojana (Endowment with Profit Policy)

The most popular form of life insurance since it makes provision for the family of the Assured in the event of early death, as also assures a lump sum amount at a desired age. The amount assured, if not paid by reason of earlier death becomes payable at the end of the endowment term. Premiums are payable for a term of years equal to the endowment term or until death if it occurs within this period. If payment of premiums is discontinued after at least two years premiums have been paid, a paid up policy for an amount bearing the same proportion to the sum assured as the number of premiums actually paid to the number stipulated for in the policy, will be automatically secured.

First Policy issue date

  • 01-06-2008

Key Feature

  • Minimum Entry Age : 16 Years
  • Maximum Entry Age : 65 Years
  • Maximum Maturity Age : 70 Years
  • Minimum Policy Term: 5 Years
  • Maximum Policy Term: 54 Years
  • Minimum Sum Assured: Rs. 25 Thousand
  • Premium Payment Mode: Yearly/Half Yearly/Quarterly

Benefits

  • Sum assured plus accrued bonus in case of the maturity
  • Sum assured plus bonus will be paid to nominated person in case of death of the assured
  • Loan facility
  • Tax benefits

Additional Benefits

  • Premium Waiver Benefit
  • Accidental Death Benefit
  • Monthly Income Benefit
  • Permanent Total Disability Benefit

This is an optional benefit which can be opted equal to sum assured or Rs. 50 Lakh whichever is less.

Critical Illness Rider Benefit

  • It covers 20 Critical illnesses.
  • This is an optional benefit which can be opted equal to sum assured or Rs. 50 Lakh whichever is less.
  • The critical illness rider amount is payable in full amount on confirmation of any of the 20 illnesses.
